About

I work at the intersection of robotics research, technical communication, and education. My research focuses on robotic manipulation, multi-object grasping, simulation, and benchmarking. Alongside research, I enjoy turning technical ideas into tutorials, structured lessons, and writing that helps people understand and apply difficult concepts.

This website brings together my research, publications, writing, teaching, and ways to work with me.
