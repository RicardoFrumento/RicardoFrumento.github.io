About this section

I publish essays, tutorials, and notes here. My goal is to write pieces that are practical, clear, and useful for students, researchers, and technical teams — especially people working in robotics, experimentation, and research-heavy environments.

Most posts will fall into one of four categories: technical tutorials, robotics explainers, research workflow notes, and reflections on graduate work when they offer something concrete and useful.

Writing approach

I care most about writing that is useful. That usually means concrete examples, plain language, and a focus on helping the reader understand how to apply an idea rather than just recognize it.

Over time, this section will also include writing samples relevant to technical writing, educational content, and research communication.

Publishing plan: I am aiming to publish one strong piece every two weeks, prioritizing depth, clarity, and usefulness over volume.
