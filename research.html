Research

Robotic manipulation, grasp planning, and research infrastructure

My work focuses on robotic manipulation, multi-object grasping, simulation workflows, and benchmarking. I am especially interested in turning hard experimental problems into clear, reproducible pipelines.

Multi-object grasping

Strategies and evaluation methods for grasping in cluttered scenes and selecting effective manipulation policies.

Benchmarking and evaluation

Open-source benchmarking and structured comparison frameworks for learning and manipulation systems.

Simulation workflows

Experiment pipelines built around Python, MuJoCo, ROS, and reproducible analysis routines.
